## 4.2 记账页面结构（HTML）

```html
<form id="add-transaction">
  <input type="number" name="amount" placeholder="金额" required>
  
  <select name="type">
    <option value="expense">支出</option>
    <option value="income">收入</option>
  </select>

  <input type="date" name="date" value="今天">

  <select name="category_main">…</select>
  <select name="category_sub">…</select>

  <select multiple name="tags">…</select>

  <select name="payment_method">
    <option value="wechat">微信</option>
    <option value="alipay_family">支付宝亲密付</option>
    <option value="bank">银行卡</option>
  </select>

  <input type="text" name="note" placeholder="备注（可选）">

  <button type="submit">记一笔</button>
</form>
```
